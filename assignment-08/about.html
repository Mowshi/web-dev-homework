Why this project?

I recently picked up this little hobby of mine where I take Hot Wheels cars and customize

them. I've found it to be a fun and inexpensive activity to do when board. I have also

replicated real life cars such as the '95 Miata in this instructable. I am mostly doing

this all on my own. What I mean by that is there are very few people who customize Hot

Wheels cars like this, so I thought making an instructable would help "raise awareness"

to this niche hobby

What does it mean for me?

As I said, I just want to help make this hobby a bit more mainsteam not only because

I enjoy it, but also because I think anyone with a limited budjet who enjoys tinkering

would get a kick out of it.
