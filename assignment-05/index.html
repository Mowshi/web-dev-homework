Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (any type will do)
  • 2 table spoons of butter
  • 1 slice of cheese (any flavor)

Directions

  1. Preheat skillet over medium heat
  2. Add one tablespoon of butter to a slice of bread and place on skillet butter side down.
  3. Place slice of cheese on top of bread. You can also add lunch meat.
  4. Butter the other slice of bread and place on sandwich butter side up.
  5. Flip snadwich over as many times as needed until golden brown on each side.
  6. Enjoy!
